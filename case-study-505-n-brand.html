The Asset

Class A office tower in the heart of Glendale.

15 stories, 334,590 SF, with prior ownership having spent ±$14M / $43 PSF on capital improvements completed in 2019 and 2025 — the building is fully improved, limiting future capital needs and basis creep.

Business Plan at Acquisition

Three things made the deal work at this basis.

  1. Class A tower at $170 PSF. Below replacement cost in a recovering Glendale submarket, with accretive seller financing from Deutsche Bank — preferred-buyer treatment because of Pendulum's track record protecting lender collateral.
  2. Recently renovated. Prior owner spent ±$14M / $43 PSF on capital, so Pendulum inherits a fully-improved asset and isn't chasing capex.
  3. Downside protection. In-place contractual cash flow with 4.58 years of WALT across institutional-grade tenants (Phonexa, CalSTRS, Korn Ferry, Wood Smith Henning & Bermann, CA State Board of Equalization).

Restaurant / lobby revitalization

Pendulum engaged Wolcott Architecture to energize the front door and lobby. Prior ownership had a dormant deli space; existing and prospective tenancy want a high-quality, all-day-café food option that doesn't currently exist in the market — and have indicated willingness to subsidize rent and build-out to recognize the office-value enhancement.

Restaurant operator agreement is signed; construction begins next quarter. The grand-open ribbon cutting is planned in conjunction with the City of Glendale. Numerous broker and marketing events have been completed to date with more scheduled in the coming months.
