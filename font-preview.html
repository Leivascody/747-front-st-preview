The Thesis

A loan-to-own play on a fully-improved Jackson Square office building.

Ares originated a $54.9M loan on the building in 2019 against a $70.5M purchase (all-in basis of $1,019 PSF). Pendulum is acquiring that loan at $46.95M — roughly 88¢ on the dollar of the original origination, and ±56% of the current owner's all-in basis.

Prior ownership and tenants have invested significant capital into cosmetic and back-of-house improvements that new ownership does not have to spend. The building sits in Jackson Square, one of San Francisco's most supply-constrained office submarkets with effectively no new competitive supply due to historic and zoning constraints.

Best-in-market product at commodity rents — first mover on rent decreases within the comp set, with an experienced ownership team executing a spec suite and amenity plan. — 747 Front St business plan thesis
